Zoom

A powerful video conferencing platform that brings people together for virtual meetings, webinars, and collaboration.

What is Zoom?

Zoom is a collaborative tool that allows students and instructors to interact in an online learning environment. Zoom is also useful in reaching out for technology support if you are having issues with instructional technology in learning environments.

Here are a few things that you can do in Zoom:

Secure Chat

Chat securely with anyone at Augusta University.

Video Conference Calls

Connect with others through high-quality video calls.

Schedule Meetings

Plan and organize meetings with colleagues and teams.
