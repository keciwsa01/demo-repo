What is Microsoft Teams?

Microsoft Teams is a collaborative tool that allows students and instructors to interact in an online learning environment. Microsoft Teams is also useful in reaching out for technology support if you are having issues with instructional technology in learning environments.

Here are a few things that you can do in Microsoft Teams:

Group Work

Edit documents real-time in one click using Office 365.

Secure Chat

Chat securely with anyone at Augusta University - just ask for their name, you don't need their phone number.

Video Conference Calls

Connect with other students in your group through video calls.

Schedule Meetings

Schedule meetings with other students to complete group work together.
